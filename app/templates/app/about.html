About StartupBD

Established in 2022, StartupBD was created to showcase Bangladesh's vibrant startup ecosystem both locally and overseas. It represents the shared interests of the startup community and unifies efforts to support the ecosystem under its various initiatives and programmes. With StartupBD, startups and ecosystem partners can more easily discover and access available avenues of support.
